Who are we?

Let them eat cake is a team of two Seattlites eating their way through the town. We met at University of Washington during our freshmen year so long ago. We quickly became great friends and started taking food journeys together. We would pick a random bus and hop on and see where it took us then ate and hopped on a returning bus back to campus.
